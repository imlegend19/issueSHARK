Welcome to issueSHARK's documentation!
======================================
**issueSHARK** collects data from several ITSs. Currently, the tool supports collecting issues, events, and comments
on issues from Jira, Bugzilla, and GitHub. The data is gathered, by accessing the REST APIs of the systems.
